About Me

Who I am

Hi, my name is Daniel and I'm a full-stack developer in the making. Driven by a fierce entrepreneurial spirit, I strive to merge my passions for technology and business into game-changing solutions.

Where I come from

My roots are spread across Europe and I am currently located in Belgium. I fluently speak 5 languages, including English, Spanish, German, French, and Italian (it sure did come to Rome!)

What I do

While I primarily run my work as a web developer in freelance fashion, I also have valuable professional experience working in fast-paced startup environments under my belt.

What I know

As a web developer, I am entirely self-taught after diving into the field at the start of 2021, all while maintaining my double degree studies in global business and international management.
